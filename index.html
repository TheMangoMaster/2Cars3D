About Us

Put your coordination skills to test by controlling 2 cars at the same time!

2 Cars 3D is a simple yet complicated game where your mental coordination skills are put to test.
Control the blue car and red car at the same time by tapping the left or right side of the screen respectively. Collect ALL spheres and avoid hitting cubes. Your goal is to get as much score as possible by collecting the spheres. Remember, missing any sphere is GAME OVER.
